About

PlatHacks is a 12-hour hackathon for highschoolers taking place in the Bay Area that focuses on using coding to create projects that attempt to solve any large societal problem, by intertwining CS with fields like biology, medicine, business and politics. This Hackathon is completely FREE, thanks to our wonderful sponsors, and no coding experience is required.
